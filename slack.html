About Slack

Slack is a type instant messaging platform, similar to WhatsApp, snapchat and messenger, developed by Slack Technologies. It is meant to help people and their teams work together more seamlessly. It was designed this way in order to help support the way people naturally work together, so that they can collaborate with other people online as efficiently as you do face-to-face. The main part of slack is called the workspace, which is made of different channels, each belonging to a different team member where communicate and talk to each as well as send messages and share files. Your even able to make private channels for conversation between just you and another individual, you can even start a voice or video call with any other members.

Registration

There are three main ways of joining a slack workspace, option 1 accept an email invitation from someone in the workspace, option 2 join your companys workspace automatically using your work email address option 3 find workspaces you're eligible to join by looking up your email address. Option one has two steps to first you have to Click Join Now on the invitation then just enter your full name and a password, then click Create Account. Option two joining automatically has a few more steps first go onto slack.com/get-started, then Click Find your workspace, then enter the approved email address, you will then get an email from slack, in the email click Confirm Email Address and finale next to your workspaces name, click Join to create an account and sign in. The third and final options finding any workspace you are eligible to join you just have to go on to slack.com/get-started and click Find your Slack workspaces. To create a slack workspace, go no to slack.com/create and enter your email address and you will get a 6- digit confirmation code, enter your code, then name your workspace, create a new channel for your workspace, add a few coworkers or friends email addresses. click See Your Channel in Slack to visit the workspace then Select Finish Signing Up at the top of the screen and enter your name and password, and finale click Finish to save.

How to use the App

Click the search bar in the upper left corner to open the search window, type whatever you’re looking for into the search bar and press enter.

Channels are used for communication with people in your workspace. You can create your own channel or join an existing one. You can use channels to talk about certain topics or work on projects. Members can join public channels if they want but have to be invited to use private ones.

Directs messages are used to send a private message to another member of the workspace. You can also create group direct messages and add any member of your workspace into it.

Slack Apps increase the power of your workspace. Using apps on Slack offers you new functions and services within one platform, it makes it easier for members to manage their workload. You can use the provided API’s to read, write and update data on Slack.

You can use the video-conference option to have discussions, share information and work on projects more efficiently with other members. Video-conferencing offers you the option to work from home with people anywhere in the world. You can also share your screen with other members during the video-conference.

How it can be used in a learning enviroment

There are many different ways for a teacher or lecturer to use social media to help teach his or her class one way they are able to use it is to post homework when they are not able to do so in class as well as informing students and their families about upcoming events. Another way it can help is if a student is not sure about something they can send them a message instead of hoping the bump into each other and ask face to face it can also be used to upload lecture videos in to group chat in facebook and twitter for people who could not come in that day. it can also be used to monitor a students progress a prime example is the use of moodle.

One way that Slack can be used to help in teaching is in the making of study groups when doing group assignments. This way groups do not have to meet on scheduled days but can also communicate and help each other in the comfort of their own home sending files and emails to one another and exchanging ideas and asking for their opinion and since it is almost instant you dont have to worry about about a lag in communication.

As I said above it can also be for things other than study groups as it allow the class teacher or lecturer included to alway be able to interact with each other even outside of school allowing the student to get the most up to date information about work and events like when are grades our out straight from the staff.
